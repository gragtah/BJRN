Cloud 9

An intelligent dating application which helps sets up dates with matches through Tinder etc., uses NLP against the chat text to determine your match’s interests, preferences, time availability, and a personal summary, etc. and recommends and schedules a date night for you at a great venue at a perfect time by leveraging Yelp + Google calendar APIs. Finally, we schedule booking an Uber for you when it’s time to leave for the date.
